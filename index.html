PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

Alpha Bravo is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

The short version: we collect anonymous usage analytics only. No personal data, no account, no cloud sync.

Data we collect

The app uses Mixpanel to collect anonymous analytics events:

  • Quiz started (whether a personal best exists at time of start)
  • Quiz completed (total time, correct count, accuracy)
  • Result shared (which share method was used)
  • Try again tapped (score at time of tap)

No name, email address, location, device identifier, or any personally identifiable information is collected or transmitted.

Data we do not collect

  • No account or sign-in is required
  • Quiz records are stored locally on your device only
  • No microphone, camera, contacts, or location access is requested

Third-party services

Analytics are processed by Mixpanel. You can review their privacy policy at mixpanel.com.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
